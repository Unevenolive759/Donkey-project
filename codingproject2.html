Donkey's relationships with other characters

Why we love Donkey

Donkey has good relationships with all of the characters in the movie. The only problem is that Shrek often get annoyed with Donkey because of how spontaneous he is.

Donkey's relationship with other characters in the movie



  • Shrek: Donkey and Shrek are very good friends. They have gone on many journeys together and even though Donkey talks like crazy and annoys Shrek they are still very good friends.
  • Fiona: Donkey and Fiona get along quite well. Donkey met Fiona when him and Shrek went to rescure her so that Shrek could get his swamp back.
